Project 7

A digital cookbook designed for seamless sharing and collaboration among friends, this innovative platform revolutionizes the way we approach cooking and recipe management. Its standout feature is the "Pantry-to-Plate" functionality, which allows users to input their available pantry items and then suggests recipes that can be made with those ingredients. This not only minimizes food waste but also sparks creativity in the kitchen.

The cookbook offers a user-friendly interface where friends can easily share their favorite recipes, add personal notes, and even customize recipes according to their dietary preferences or restrictions. Each user's profile becomes a personalized cookbook, reflecting their tastes and cooking habits.

SAnother remarkable feature is the ability to plan meals together. Friends can collaborate on meal plans, making it easier to organize potlucks or shared meals. This feature integrates with a calendar for better meal planning and even suggests shopping lists based on the chosen recipes, ensuring everyone knows what to bring for a shared cooking experience.

Lastly, the digital cookbook includes a feature for rating and reviewing recipes, enabling a community-driven approach to discovering and refining dishes. This aspect fosters a sense of community among users, as they can rely on trusted opinions from friends and fellow food enthusiasts. In summary, this digital cookbook is a comprehensive platform that not only stores recipes but also encourages collaboration, creativity, and efficient meal planning, all tailored to the user's pantry and personal network.
